First Time We Meet

06 Nov 2022

We first met in the comfort of our homes for our arranged marriage meeting. We were bit apprehensive at first, but as soon as we started talking, those nerves quickly dissipated. We talked about our backgrounds, families, and aspirations, and I was impressed by your passion for your work and your strong sense of family values. As the call came to an end, I felt a sense of comfort and ease with you, and I knew that I was looking forward to the next time we would have the opportunity to talk.

Family Final Meeting

27 Nov 2022

The final meeting with our families was the culmination of our arranged marriage process. We sat together, surrounded by our loved ones, as they got to know each other and asked questions to ensure that this union was the right fit for both of us. I was impressed by the ease with which you interacted with my family, and I could see that they were equally taken with you. The atmosphere was light and positive, and by the end of the meeting, there was a mutual understanding and excitement for our future together. Our families agreed that we were well-suited for each other and gave their blessings for our union. It was a special moment that we will always cherish, and I am grateful for the love and support of our families as we embark on this new journey together.

Our Engagement Day

04 Feb 2023

Our engagement day was a memorable and joyous occasion. We stood together, surrounded by our close friends and family, as we made a public commitment to each other. The love and support of those closest to us was palpable, and I felt so grateful to have you by my side. The day was filled with laughter, love, and happy tears as we exchanged rings and pledged our love and commitment to each other. I will never forget the look in your eyes as we promised to stand by each other, through thick and thin, for the rest of our lives. The memories from that day will stay with me forever, and I am so thankful for the love and happiness that we share.
